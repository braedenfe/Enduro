What We Use

THE Fibres

01

Merino Wool

Ultra-fine 17.5–18.5 micron merino. Temperature-regulating, naturally odour-resistant, and softer against skin than any synthetic. Performs from dawn trails to post-run commutes.

Thermoregulating Anti-odour ZQ Certified Biodegradable
02

Organic Cotton

GOTS-certified, grown without pesticides or synthetic fertilisers. Exceptional softness, breathability, and durability. The foundation of our gym-focused range.

GOTS Certified OEKO-TEX Hypoallergenic Durable
03

Bamboo Blend

Extraordinarily soft, naturally antibacterial, and moisture-wicking. Bamboo grows without irrigation at remarkable speed — the most renewable fibre we source.

Antibacterial Moisture-wicking Renewable UV Protective
04

Hemp Fibre

The strongest natural fibre on earth. Carbon-negative to grow, UV-protective to wear, and only gets better with every wash. The backbone of our technical running pieces.

Carbon Negative UPF 40+ Strongest Fibre Improves with Age
05

Tencel™

Lyocell derived from sustainably sourced eucalyptus wood pulp. Exceptionally smooth against skin, naturally moisture-managing, and produced in a closed-loop process that recycles water and solvents.

Closed-loop Process Moisture-managing OEKO-TEX Biodegradable
